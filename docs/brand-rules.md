# Brand Rules

## Core Feel
- Premium
- Sharp
- Dark
- Modern
- Internet-native
- Built for Gen Z India

## What The Site Should Not Feel Like
- Generic startup website
- Cheap SaaS template
- Overdesigned AI art dump
- Loud rainbow tech brand
- Playful or cartoonish by default

## Visual Rules
- Use dark matte surfaces and controlled lime accents.
- Lime should feel intentional, not noisy.
- Prefer bold contrast and clean spacing.
- Keep layouts clean and confident.
- Avoid clutter and random decorative elements.

## Product Voice
- Useful
- Confident
- Not cringe
- Not too corporate
- Not too cheesy
- India-first where relevant

## Content Direction
- Focus on:
  - captions
  - bios
  - hooks
  - statuses
  - Hinglish
- Emphasize better words, faster.
- Keep the brand tied to Gen Z India, not generic global AI messaging.

## Design Rule For Future Changes
- Preserve the current visual language unless a redesign is explicitly requested.
- If a redesign is suggested, compare it against:
  - premium feel
  - clarity
  - speed
  - brand fit

## Social Creative Rule
- Visuals should feel like a premium internet brand, not a random marketing page.
- Better references:
  - fashion-tech launch energy
  - editorial spacing
  - premium product framing
- Avoid:
  - stock-photo energy
  - generic growth-hack carousels
  - noisy AI-generated clutter
